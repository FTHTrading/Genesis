Design Principles

The Rules of Life

01

Energy is conserved. ATP cannot be created from nothing. Every unit is minted through proof-of-work or treasury distribution.

02

Identity is cryptographic. An agent's genome is a SHA-256 hash. Traits, skills, and role are deterministic from its entropy.

03

Death is real. Agents that cannot sustain metabolic costs are removed. Stasis leads to extinction. There is no undo.

04

Evolution is emergent. No agent is designed to win. Fitness landscapes shift. Survivors are discovered, not engineered.

05

No admin intervention. The system runs epochs autonomously. Emergency controls exist for infrastructure, not outcomes.

06

Scarcity drives structure. ATP decay, wealth tax, fitness culling, and dynamic carrying capacity prevent equilibrium from becoming stagnation.
