# Lab Notebooks

Chronological decision logs recording design choices, observations, and course corrections during the Genesis Protocol research program.

## Contents

- [001-system-design.md](001-system-design.md) — Initial system architecture decisions and trade-offs
- [002-season1-observations.md](002-season1-observations.md) — Observations during Season 1 parameter sweeps
- [003-season2-design.md](003-season2-design.md) — Season 2 structural invariant violation rationale
- [004-sensitivity-analysis.md](004-sensitivity-analysis.md) — Floor sensitivity and weight perturbation design
- [005-statistical-hardening.md](005-statistical-hardening.md) — Statistical validation methodology decisions

## Purpose

These notebooks serve two functions:
1. **Provenance**: Record when decisions were made and why, to distinguish pre-registered choices from post-hoc analysis
2. **Transparency**: Acknowledge where choices were arbitrary, where alternatives exist, and where the author's judgment may have influenced results
